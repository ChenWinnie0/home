Oil Paint

Oil paint is an art medium that uses slow-drying paint based in drying oil. It is often very thick and can hold it's shape, providing opportunities for 3 dimensional aspects in art created by it.

Famous Artists

Vincent Van Gogh

Vincent Willem van Gogh, possibly one of the most famous artists that are still relavent today, was a Dutch painter, specializing in post-impressionism. He created more than 2,000 works, at least 860 of which were oil paintings. He painted landscapes, portraits, still-lifes, and many other types of art. Learn More

Johannes Vermeer

Johannes Vermeer also known as Jan Vermeer, was a Dutch painter, specializing in interior landscapes and scenes. He is considered one of the best painters of the Dutch Golden age, and some of his paintings are still popular today. Learn More
